ABOUT ME

I've always been a builder.


I like to be hands on. I learn best by doing, and I try and bring that philosophy to every project that I take on. Creating something with others and working in a collaborative environment is a true joy for me. Being around people motivates me and their energy pushes me forward. Good design and a quality user experience is my focus for each project that I take on. When not coding, I enjoy woodworking, riding my bike, and playing Scrabble.
